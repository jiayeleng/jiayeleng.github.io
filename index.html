About Me

Hi, I'm Jiaye, a third-year Ph.D. candidate at City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK), working with Prof. Hongbo Fu and Prof. Miu Ling Lam. Currently my research focuses on Human-AI Interaction, Creativity Support, and VR/AR. During my studies, I closely collaborated with Prof. Hui Ye and Prof. Pengfei Xu. Previously, I obtained my master's degree from Beihang University (BUAA), supervised by Prof. Lili Wang.
